PYTHONISTA

INTRODUCTION

  • Python is one of the world's most popular programming languages.
  • Python is a popular general-purpose programming language. It is used in machine learning, web development, desktop applications, and many other fields.
  • Considering all this, ACES decided to organize a workshop on Basics of Python. It was a 2 days free workshop; open to all branches of PVG's COET.
  • The workshop was arranged for 2 days (28th and 29th January 2023).

DAY-1

  • The session was conducted by Shyam Lokhande.
  • Session 1 was planned in such a way that even amateurs could understand.
  • . The topics covered are as follows : Introduction to python, Syntax, Comments, Variables, Data Types, Operators, Conditional Statements, Loops, Built in Datatypes
  • Students were also assigned with some problems to try which made the session more interesting.

DAY-2

  • The session was conducted by Shivendra Deshpande.
  • On day 2 competitive coding problems on HackerRank were solved along with explaination and approach..

SUMMARY

It was a very interactive session. As they were being taught by their seniors, students could ask their doubts freely.
The session provided the students something which could not be achieved through conventional classroom teaching.
After the session, students were confident and had a command over the language. The session was very helpful for the students to clear their basics and gave a kickstart towards advanced coding.
